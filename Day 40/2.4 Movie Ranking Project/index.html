My top 3 movies of all-time.

Taare Zameen par

This is my favourite movie. Showcasing a story of a dyslexic child and how he overcomes his fears.

3 Idiots

Really cool movie which revolve around three Engineering students which make you fall in love with your work not the outcome

Madaari

Super beautiful film. It describes how a common man's anger can lead to chaos
